COMPANY PROFILE

YTY's mission for over 30 years has been to keep frontline employees safe through the manufacture of high-quality, single-use Examination gloves. Our original corporate ethos around quality, service and value continues to drive us today, making us one of the largest manufacturers of single-use Examination gloves in the world.

The management of YTY Group recognizes and accepts its responsibility for the protection of the environment, health and safety of our personnel in the performance of our services.

YTY Group's employees continually strive toward the goal of a perfect health and safety record of zero incidents.
